About Me

Hi, I'm Mahika! I’ve always been fascinated by space and the boundless opportunities technology provides to explore and understand the universe. As a Computer Engineering student, I’m passionate about how technology can not only solve real-world problems but also spark innovation and shape the future. I’m particularly excited about the potential of emerging technologies and how they can transform industries and everyday life.

Dance has been an important part of my life, offering a creative outlet for expression and movement. While my main focus is on technology, I continue to find inspiration in dance, especially in how both technology and dance can communicate complex ideas and emotions in unique ways. I enjoy blending my interests in tech, space, and dance to create meaningful and innovative experiences.
